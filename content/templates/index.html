Who we are

Dragonfly brings together talented specialists who have excelled in their fields. Our technical skills include statistical analysis, database design and administration, data visualisation, and software development.

We work on the real-world problems that matter to our clients, bringing solid, reliable evidence to the conversation. Data science is all about getting to the bottom of it, and we are happy to dive deep. Our approach is fluid and pragmatic. We have the technical skills to pick problems apart, and the creativity to find novel solutions.
