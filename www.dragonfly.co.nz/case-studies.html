Design and development of a reporting system for the Coromandel scallop fishery

New Zealand scallops

Seafood Industry Council

Dragonfly Science set up a reporting system for the New Zealand Seafood Industry Council. We provided:

  • a simple and robust web-based data collection and entry process
  • client-focused outputs, including graphical analysis
  • a secure database for long term information storage

Reducing seabird fatalities in trawl fisheries

Sarah Wilcox

We designed an experiment to find the best mitigation device for albatross. Our work included:

Risk analysis of fisheries to seabirds

Yellow-eyed penguin

Some rights reserved, Richard Giddins

A risk index was calculated for 64 species of New Zealand seabirds, to enable population management decisions to be made. The project involved:

  • pragmatic use of limited available information
  • statistical analysis
  • preparing graphical information and reports for decision-makers
